The GCC insurance landscape spans 6 markets with 150+ licensed carriers and a combined GWP of $28.6B in 2026. Digital transformation is accelerating but uneven — UAE and Saudi Arabia lead with mature online ecosystems while Kuwait and Oman are still building foundational digital journeys. The competitive battleground is shifting from price to digital experience, product breadth, and AI-driven personalisation.
