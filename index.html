GCC Insurance Market: Overview 2026
📋 Data sources: Market size figures are sourced from Alpen Capital GCC Insurance Industry Report, May 2026 (GCC total, Qatar); Milliman KSA Year-End 2025 Monitor (Saudi Arabia); CBUAE / Atlas Magazine 2025 (UAE); Atlas Magazine March 2026 (Oman); The Report Cube 2025 (Kuwait, Bahrain). Digital Maturity Index scores and online channel share percentages are internal estimates based on product coverage and UX audits — not official regulator statistics.

The GCC insurance market reached approximately $48.5B in GWP in 2025, growing at an annualised 4.9% to reach a projected $61.8B by 2030, according to Alpen Capital. Saudi Arabia and UAE dominate, together accounting for over 80% of regional premium. Digital transformation is accelerating but uneven — UAE and Saudi Arabia lead with mature online ecosystems while Kuwait and Oman are still building foundational digital journeys.
