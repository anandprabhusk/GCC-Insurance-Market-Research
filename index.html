GCC Insurance Market: Overview 2026

The GCC insurance landscape spans 6 markets with 150+ licensed carriers. Digital transformation remains uneven: UAE and Saudi Arabia lead with mature online ecosystems, while Kuwait and Oman are still building foundational digital journeys. The battleground is shifting from price competition to digital experience, product breadth, and AI-driven personalisation.
