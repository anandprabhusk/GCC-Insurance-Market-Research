GCC Insurance Market: Overview 2026

📋 Data stack: Alpen Capital GCC Insurance Industry Report 2026; Milliman KSA Year-End 2025; CBUAE market releases; QCB, CBB, CMA Oman annual data; AXCO market profiles; internal digital audits completed June–July 2026 across product, claims, app, payment, GEO/SEO, and platform maturity.

The GCC insurance market reached approximately $48.5B and remains one of the world's most under-digitised insurance regions outside motor. UAE and Saudi Arabia dominate scale and digital maturity, while Kuwait and Oman remain constrained by legacy platforms, weaker self-service journeys, and thin Arabic-first user experience. Across the region, the largest growth upside is not in core motor but in the missing digital product shelf.

🥇 Regional Leaders

  • UAE leads on digital product breadth, claims UX, home insurance, and ecosystem depth
  • KSA leads on Arabic-first demand, health scale, and strongest AI underwriting trajectory
  • Both are the only GCC markets approaching enterprise-grade insurer cloud maturity

🥈 Mid-Tier Markets

  • Qatar benefits from QIC-led maturity but lacks aggregator competition and broader ecosystem depth
  • Bahrain has the best insurtech regulatory setup relative to its market size but low commercial scale
  • Both can leapfrog with faster execution on embedded and AI-discoverable products

🥉 Markets Most at Risk

  • Kuwait and Oman remain weakest on mobile speed, AI readiness, claims transparency, and self-service depth
  • Both markets have outsized upside because competition is weaker and first-mover advantage is still open
  • Legacy core systems remain the main bottleneck to product innovation
