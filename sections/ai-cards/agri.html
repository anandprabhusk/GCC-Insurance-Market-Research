02
Weather Index Insurance (Temperature/Rainfall)
Parametric insurance paying out when temperature exceeds 50°C or rainfall falls below defined thresholds — verified via ground stations & satellite. Critical for GCC farmers & date palm producers. Auto-payout, no claim forms.
Pioneer: Swiss Re Agri, Munich Re Weather, Ariel Re  •  GCC: Oman Agriculture pilot

Livestock Disease Detection AI
AI analyses drone imagery & IoT ear-tag biometrics (temperature, movement) to detect livestock disease early → triggers veterinary intervention & mortality insurance payout. Camel & dairy farming is significant GCC agri sector.
Pioneer: Allflex, SenseHub, Connecterra  •  GCC: None yet

Food Supply Chain Disruption Parametric
AI monitors global food commodity prices, shipping disruptions & port congestion → triggers parametric payout for food importers when supply chain index breaches threshold. GCC imports 85%+ of food — unique vulnerability.
