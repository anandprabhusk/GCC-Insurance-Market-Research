🇧🇭 Bahrain — Insurance Market 2026

Bahrain's insurance market reached $0.8B GWP in 2026, growing 4.5% YoY. Regulated by the Central Bank of Bahrain (CBB) under Resolution No. 23 of 2014, Bahrain hosts the most open regulatory environment in the GCC — including a dedicated FinTech Bay and CBB regulatory sandbox that has accelerated insurtech pilots. Despite being a smaller market, digital maturity at 60% ranks 3rd in GCC. Solidarity Group leads on product breadth; ARIG leads on reinsurance and technical innovation.

Bahrain insight: Bahrain punches above its weight digitally thanks to CBB's progressive regulatory stance and the FinTech Bay ecosystem. Solidarity leads the market but the gap to #2 is wide. The CBB sandbox is a unique accelerator for cyber and embedded insurance — no other GCC regulator offers equivalent fast-track licensing. Carriers that leverage this first-mover window will define the next 5 years of Bahrain's digital insurance market.
