The GCC Insurance GEO Playbook: 30-60-90 day framework

🟥 Days 1–30: Technical foundation

  • ✅ Implement JSON-LD Schema: InsuranceAgency, Product, FAQPage, Organization
  • ✅ Fix Core Web Vitals: target LCP <2s, pass CLS on all product pages
  • ✅ Enable HTTPS, hreflang (ar/en), canonical tags on all pages
  • ✅ Submit sitemaps (Arabic + English separately) to Search Console
  • ✅ Create or update Wikipedia brand page with sources
  • ✅ Set up Google Business Profile and Apple Maps listing per branch
  • ✅ Audit and fix broken internal links, missing meta descriptions

🟧 Days 31–60: Content authority

  • ✅ Publish bilingual (Arabic + English) answer-format guides: "What is third-party insurance in UAE?", "ما هو التأمين الصحي في السعودية?"
  • ✅ Create dedicated FAQ pages per product with 15–20 Q&As in structured HTML
  • ✅ Add expert author bylines: actuary, medical director, legal adviser
  • ✅ Publish at least one original data piece: survey or claims trends report
  • ✅ Launch premium calculator / estimator tool embedded on product landing pages
  • ✅ Create city-level landing pages (e.g. "car insurance Dubai", "تأمين سيارات الرياض")

🟢 Days 61–90: Brand authority & AI citation

  • ✅ Pitch brand to Arabic media publications for editorial coverage (Zawya, Al Arabiya biz, Gulf Business)
  • ✅ Publish press releases with data/insight that journalists and AI models will cite
  • ✅ Build links from .gov.ae, .com.sa, QFC.qa, CBB.bh regulatory/partner sites
  • ✅ Launch YouTube channel: Arabic-first explainer videos with transcripts
  • ✅ Create Perplexity-optimised content: direct Q&A answers with citations to official sources
  • ✅ Monitor AI citation score weekly using Brandwatch / SEMrush AI Visibility tracker
  • ✅ Build WhatsApp-linked landing pages: reduce friction from AI answer → conversion

🤖 Days 91+: Agentic & voice search readiness

  • ✅ Build an LLM-ready knowledge base (structured, entity-linked content at scale)
  • ✅ Register brand on AI data sources: Crunchbase, OpenCorporates, Wikidata
  • ✅ Expose product data as structured API (JSON-LD on product pages = machine-readable)
  • ✅ Optimise for voice: natural language, Arabic dialect-friendly content
  • ✅ Create an AI model-facing /llms.txt file (emerging standard for AI crawler access)
  • ✅ Deploy RAG-based internal chatbot trained on product & FAQ content for consistent AI answers
  • ✅ Track "Share of AI Voice" — how often your brand appears in AI insurance answers vs competitors
