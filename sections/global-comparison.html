Global benchmark summary: The GCC at ~2.2% GDP penetration (Alpen Capital 2026) sits far below the global average of 7.4%. Closing even half this gap to 4% GDP would represent an additional ~$40B in premium. Takaful (~$14.5B, 14% CAGR), telematics (<2% vs 30% USA), cyber (~$180M vs $15B USA), and embedded insurance are the four structural gaps where GCC carriers have the highest ROI digital investment opportunity in 2026–2028.
