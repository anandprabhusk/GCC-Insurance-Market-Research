🔎 Key Heatmap Findings

🔴 Biggest gaps region-wide

  • Cyber: 0 out of 30 carriers offer cyber digitally — 100% whitespace
  • SME bundles: <5% of carriers have any SME online journey
  • Life direct: Only Al Rajhi Takaful (KSA) has full life digital
  • Home insurance: Available in UAE only at scale; absent in KW/OM
  • Health full journey: Bupa Arabia + Daman are exceptions; rest partial

🟢 Most complete carriers

  • Tawuniya (KSA) 8.9 — Broadest product digital suite in GCC
  • Bupa Arabia (KSA) 8.7 — Unmatched health digital depth
  • Daman Health (UAE) 8.6 — Health specialist leader
  • AXA Gulf (UAE) 8.1 — Best multi-line digital breadth
  • Solidarity (BH) 7.8 — Best in class outside KSA/UAE

📈 GCC total GWP growth

  • GCC aggregate GWP grew from $20.3B (2022) to $28.6B (2026)
  • CAGR of 8.9% over 4 years — outpacing global insurance avg
  • KSA driving 52% of total GCC GWP in 2026
  • UAE contributes 32% of GCC GWP despite being 2nd market
  • Oman fastest % growth potential from lowest base
Heatmap insight: Cyber insurance is the single most significant product gap across all 30 carriers and all 6 GCC markets — a $0 digital addressable market today representing an estimated $1.8B opportunity by 2028. Motor and travel are the most digitally mature lines. Life, SME, and home insurance remain heavily offline in 4 of 6 GCC markets.
