🆕 6 Strategic Improvement Clusters

1︎ Core System Modernisation

💰 Cost: $2–8M  |  ROI horizon: 3–5 yrs  |  Complexity: ⚫ High
  • 🔴 Replace legacy monolithic policy admin (most carriers 15–25 yr old platforms)
  • 🔴 Migrate to cloud-native API-first cores (Guidewire Cloud, Majesco, Sapiens)
  • 🟠 Microservices for independent product/channel scaling
  • 🟠 Decouple distribution from policy admin for omni-channel flexibility
  • 🟩 Real-time data streaming (Kafka/Kinesis) for live risk signals
  • 🟩 Event-driven architecture for claims automation

2︎ Customer Experience Overhaul

💰 Cost: $300K–1.5M  |  NPS lift: +22 pts  |  Complexity: ⬤ Medium
  • 🔴 Fully self-serve journey: quote → bind → pay → certificate in <3 min
  • 🔴 End-to-end digital claims: FNOL online, photo upload, real-time tracker
  • 🟠 Arabic-first UX with RTL-optimised design and Arabic NLP search
  • 🟠 WhatsApp-native journeys (91% GCC users on WhatsApp)
  • 🟠 Mobile app sub-2s load, biometric login, in-app policy management
  • 🟩 Proactive push: renewal alerts, claim updates, health reminders
  • 🟩 Live chat + AI chatbot with human escalation

3︎ Product Innovation

💰 Cyber TAM: ~$500M by 2028  |  First-mover window: 12–18 months
  • 🔴 Launch retail cyber (SME + individual) — ~$180M GCC market, near-zero supply
  • 🔴 Build wellness-reward health (Vitality/AIA model)
  • 🔴 Parametric travel: auto-payout on flight delay, cancellation
  • 🟠 Income protection & critical illness as standalone digital products
  • 🟠 SME bundle (property + liability + cyber) in <5 min online
  • 🟠 On-demand/daily motor for weekend drivers, tourists
  • 🟩 Family Takaful with digital savings dashboard
  • 🟩 Pet insurance as digital-native (UAE/KSA pet ownership rising)

4︎ Data & AI Strategy

💰 Claims savings: 15–25%  |  Underwriting accuracy: +18% (Accenture)
  • 🔴 Unified CDP to consolidate siloed data
  • 🔴 AI underwriting: risk scoring with alternative data
  • 🟠 Computer vision motor damage assessment (<48 hr claims)
  • 🟠 Fraud detection ML (15–20% of GCC motor claims fraudulent)
  • 🟠 NLP claims processing: auto-extract docs, classify claims
  • 🟠 Predictive churn ML + personalised retention offers
  • 🟩 GenAI for policy Q&A, customer service bot, agent assist
  • 🟩 Arabic LLM fine-tuning for GCC insurance content

5︎ Distribution & Ecosystem

💰 Embedded CAGR: 28% (2025–2028)  |  Dev portal: $150–400K
  • 🔴 Open API layer for embedded insurance (banks, fintechs, e-commerce)
  • 🔴 Developer portal with sandbox for partner embedding
  • 🟠 Super-app presence: Al Rajhi, STC Pay, OPay, Careem
  • 🟠 White-label insurance for banking partners (BaaS → IaaS)
  • 🟠 Aggregator API: Tameeni, yallacompare, souqalmal
  • 🟩 Insurtech accelerator / co-innovation for proprietary AI

6︎ Talent & Organisation

💰 Arabic ML engineers in GCC insurance: <200 qualified  |  Talent gap: Critical
  • 🔴 Hire / retrain: data scientists, ML engineers, digital PMs
  • 🔴 Cross-functional squads (product + tech + actuarial) for agile delivery
  • 🟠 Invest in Arabic AI/NLP — severely scarce in GCC insurance
  • 🟠 University partnerships: KAUST, NYU Abu Dhabi, CMU Qatar
  • 🟩 Internal UX/UI design capability (Arabic-first)
  • 🟩 CDO empowered at board level
