🆕 6 Strategic Improvement Clusters
🤖 AI & Automation
Deploy AI across the full value chain

Chatbots, fraud detection, predictive pricing, automated claims triage, and smart renewal engines. Estimated uplift: 15–25% cost reduction.

High Priority2026–2027
📱 Digital Self-Service
End-to-end digital customer journey

Quote, buy, manage, and claim entirely online with no agent required. Mobile-first design, Arabic UX, and instant policy issuance.

CriticalQ1 2026
📈 Embedded & Parametric
New distribution & product models

Embed insurance at point-of-sale (e-commerce, automotive, travel). Parametric products for weather, agriculture, and SME business interruption.

High PriorityQ4 2026
👥 Customer Experience
Personalisation & retention

360° customer profiles, behavioural analytics, personalised product recommendations, loyalty programmes, and proactive risk alerts.

High Priority2026–2027
🔗 Open Ecosystem
API-first & partner integrations

Publish open APIs for aggregators, InsurTechs, banks, and healthcare providers. Create a marketplace of add-on coverages and services.

Medium2027
📔 Regulatory & Compliance
Digital compliance & reporting

Automated SAMA/CMA/QFC/CBB regulatory reporting, PDPL-compliant data handling, digital KYC/AML, and real-time risk dashboards for regulators.
