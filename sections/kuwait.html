🇰🇼 Kuwait — Insurance Market 2026

Kuwait's insurance market stands at $1.1B GWP in 2026, growing 3.9% YoY — the slowest growth rate in the GCC. The market is regulated by the Insurance Regulatory Unit (IRU) under the Ministry of Commerce. With 25+ carriers competing in a relatively small market, digital investment has lagged. Motor and health dominate volume, but fewer than half of carriers offer any meaningful online journey. Kuwait presents the largest digital leap opportunity in the northern GCC.

Legacy on-premise tech stacks are the primary barrier to progress.
