🇴🇲 Oman — Insurance Market 2026

Oman's insurance market is the smallest in the GCC at $0.3B GWP in 2026, growing 4.2% YoY. Regulated by the Capital Market Authority (CMA), the market has 21+ carriers competing in a nascent digital environment. Oman Vision 2040 and the CMA's digital transformation agenda are catalysing investment. Sukoon Insurance (formerly Oman Insurance) leads on digital capability. With an 8% online channel share, Oman represents the most untapped digital insurance market in the GCC — and the earliest-stage opportunity for first movers.

Oman insight: Oman is the GCC's largest digital insurance greenfield. Sukoon leads but its advantage is thin. Arabic UX is the worst in the region despite Arabic being the dominant search language. The CMA's digital mandate and Vision 2040 framework provide regulatory tailwinds no other GCC market currently has. The first carrier to invest seriously in Arabic UX, health digital, and mobile will capture outsized market share in a low-competition digital environment.
