Opportunity insight: The GCC digital insurance market is projected to grow from $5.8B in digital premium today to $13.9B by 2028 — a $8.1B incremental opportunity. Cyber insurance offers the highest percentage growth from near-zero base. Health and motor offer the largest absolute dollar opportunities. The first-mover matrix shows KSA and UAE hold both the largest markets and the most untapped digital gaps in high-value lines like cyber, SME, home, and life — making them the definitive strategic priority for any serious digital insurance investment in 2026–2027.
