Technology Landscape
🏭 Cloud & Hosting

UAE leads with AWS and Azure multi-cloud. KSA increasingly adopts local AWS Riyadh region. Qatar, Kuwait, and Oman mostly rely on on-premise or regional data-centres. Bahrain benefits from AWS Bahrain region.

AWSAzureGoogle CloudOn-PremiseOracle Cloud
🛠 Core Insurance Platforms

Legacy COBOL/Oracle-based cores dominate older carriers. Newer entrants use Majesco, Guidewire, and Duck Creek. Insurtechs build proprietary micro-service stacks. SAP FS-CD widely used in KSA government-linked insurers.

GuidewireMajescoDuck CreekSAP FS-CDOracle
🤖 AI & Automation

Chatbots are common at UAE tier-1 carriers. KSA deploying NLP-based claims triage. Most GCC markets still use rule-based automation rather than ML. Telematics-based UBI policies rare outside UAE.
