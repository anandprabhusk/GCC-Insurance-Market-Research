🌍 Insurance Product Gaps: GCC vs Global Markets

GCC insurance penetration averaged just 1.5–1.9% of GDP — against a global average of 7.4% and emerging-market average of 3.4%. This structural under-insurance reflects not just awareness gaps, but a missing product shelf: entire categories that are standard globally are virtually absent in GCC digital storefronts.

Global benchmark insight: In the UK, US and EU, consumers can buy cyber, life, parametric, pet, gig, wellness-linked and embedded insurance entirely online in minutes. In the GCC, fewer than 5% of insurers offer even basic life protection digitally. The gap is not demand-side — it is a product design, distribution and regulatory gap that represents the largest single growth opportunity in GCC financial services for 2026–2030.
