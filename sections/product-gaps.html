🌍 GCC Insurance Product Gaps vs Global Regions

GCC insurance penetration averaged just 1.9% of GDP in 2020 — far below the global average of 7.4% and even the emerging-market average of 3.4%. The Alpen Capital 2026 report projects GCC GWP to grow from US$48.5B (2025) to US$61.8B by 2030 at 4.9% CAGR — but structural product gaps versus mature markets remain wide and represent the highest-value opportunity in the region.

Bottom line: Life protection, cyber, parametric, wellness-linked and on-demand insurance are the five biggest product white spaces in GCC — absent even at the most digitally advanced carriers. Any insurer or insurtech launching even one of these with a clean online journey in 2026 faces near-zero direct digital competition in the region.
