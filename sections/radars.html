📊 Radar Findings — Key Patterns

🧠 AI is the universal weak spot

  • No carrier scores above 8.5 on AI & Automation
  • Even GCC leaders (Tawuniya, Bupa) lack generative AI or predictive underwriting
  • AI gap widens dramatically below the top 3 carriers per country
  • Kuwait and Oman market leaders score 5.0 on AI — essentially zero deployment
  • AI is the single highest-leverage investment for any GCC carrier in 2026–2027

📷 Mobile is the strongest dimension

  • Tawuniya 9.5 mobile score — highest single dimension across all carriers
  • KSA and UAE leaders have best-in-class native apps with 4.3+ store ratings
  • Mobile is where digital investment has been most consistently deployed
  • Sharp mobile cliff: top-3 per country strong; everyone else has basic or no app
  • Flutter and React Native dominate as preferred cross-platform choices

📊 SEO & GEO systematically under-invested

  • No carrier scores above 9.0 on SEO & GEO — capped by Arabic content absence
  • Bupa Arabia 7.5 SEO despite being health market leader — significant gap
  • GEO (AI search visibility) pulls all carrier scores down 1–2 points
  • Carriers invest in ads but not organic — high cost, low sustainability
  • First carrier per country to crack Arabic GEO will gain outsized share
Radar insight: The shape of a best-in-class GCC carrier (Tawuniya) is a near-full hexagon except for a clear AI dip. Every other market leader shows an asymmetric shape — either strong mobile + weak AI, or strong claims + weak SEO. This reveals that no single GCC carrier has cracked all 6 dimensions simultaneously, meaning the “complete digital insurer” position is still wide open across every market.
