Anoud Technologies is a GCC-born and GCC-built insurance technology company headquartered in Kuwait, with active deployments across KSA, UAE, Kuwait, Bahrain, and Oman. It is purpose-built for MENA insurance regulatory requirements, Arabic-first UX, and full Takaful compliance — making it uniquely positioned in the GCC mid-market where global platforms face localisation and cost barriers.

🔌
Core Platform Suite
Full P&C + Life + Health + Takaful policy administration, claims management, billing, reinsurance, and broker management — all in a single integrated platform with Arabic-first UI and RTL support.
🏫
Regulatory Compliance
Pre-built compliance for SAMA (KSA), CBUAE (UAE), CBK (Kuwait), CBO (Oman), and CBB (Bahrain) — including mandatory IFRS 17 reporting, Takaful fund separation, and local regulatory filing formats.
🌐
GCC Market Footprint
Deployed across KSA, UAE, Kuwait, Bahrain, and Oman — covering both conventional and Takaful carriers, with dedicated implementation teams in each market.
📱
Digital Channels
Web portal, agent portal, mobile app framework, and broker portal — all natively Arabic-first with optional English toggle. Supports online quoting, issuance, renewal, and claims FNOL.
🔗
Integration & APIs
REST API layer supporting integration with aggregators (Tameeni, Yallacompare), national health systems (Wasfaty, UIIS), payment gateways (STC Pay, Apple Pay), and reinsurance platforms.
⚠
Gaps vs Global Platforms
Smaller global SI partner ecosystem vs Guidewire. Cloud-native SaaS maturity still evolving. AI/ML native capabilities limited vs Majesco CustomerEngage. Less suited to mega-carrier Tier 1 global deployments.
